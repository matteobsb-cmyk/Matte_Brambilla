01 / The Tour

A van across Italy.

To launch the hacker house, we organized a national tour across Italy in a van — visiting 6 cities and hosting hackathons, lectures, and hackerspaces.

We engaged 600+ builders and visited key innovation hubs like the Apple Developer Academy in Naples and the Bending Spoons offices, alongside fantastic guests such as Alberto Sangiovanni-Vincentelli.

600+ builders·6 cities·~20 events

Start · Genova

Cast off from Genova

The tour set sail from Genova — the van boarded the ferry and headed south to Sicily.

01 · Catania

Fireside chat with Sabrina Maniscalco

CEO & founder of Algorithmiq, Italy's leading quantum-computing startup.

02 · Napoli

Hackerspace @ Apple Developer Academy

A hacker space where builders shipped their own projects, closing with final demos and a fireside chat on startups & fundraising.

03 · Bologna

Fireside chat with Luca Foresti

Serial entrepreneur, now CEO of Niulinx — a deep-tech startup bringing autonomous driving to European roads.

04 · Roma

Hackathon

100 builders, one day of building on projects or research, a €50K prize pool, with Codex and 8 Italian startups.

05 · Torino

Lecture by Alberto Sangiovanni-Vincentelli

Pioneer of Electronic Design Automation and co-founder of Cadence & Synopsys (~$200B combined).

06 · Milano

Fireside chat with Valentina Jerusalmi

General Manager at Bending Spoons (Mosaic, AOL): scaling, acquisitions and operational execution. Home of the house — the final stop.
